Private equity investment

        We specialize in the most attractive assets in China, continue to follow the leading business in segments which have sustainable competitiveness, and grow with the invested companies together through value-adding.

        We are to build a distinctive and experienced professional investment team, and mainly invest in growing business, with invested sectors concentrating on advanced manufacturing and massive consumption; we keep following innovation and upgrading of traditional business, and are committed to exploring business modes and technology innovation within these sectors; meanwhile, we detect investment opportunities in integration of TMT and offline sectors, formulate investment planning for NEEQ sectors, and particularly pay attention to chances arising from integration of internet and traditional sectors.
