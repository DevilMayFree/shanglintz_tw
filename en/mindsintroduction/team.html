Top management team

ZHOU Yunjie

President

        Mr. ZHOU Yunjie, Chinese national, 54 years old, has Australian permanent residency, and graduated from the PBC School of Finance at Tsinghua University with an EMBA. As one of the outstanding entrepreneurs in Beijing, Mr. ZHOU Yunjie concurrently is President of ORG Packaging Shareholding Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as “ORG”) share-held by ShanghaiSL Investment Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as “ShanghaiSL”), and also takes the social positions including Executive Member of the Beijing Municipal Federation of Industry & Commerce, Executive Director of China Packaging Federation and Director in Metal Containers Committee of China Packaging Federation and etc.

        Mr. ZHOU Yunjie has been devoted to the relevant work of the metal packaging industry of China, and deeply involved in the development of the packaging industry. Since the 90s, ORG, under the leadership of Mr. ZHOU Yunjie, has become the leading player in the metal packaging industry. Being the integrated solution provider in the metal packaging products, ORG is the largest metal packaging company listed in A-share. Mr. ZHOU Yunjie, with his global vision and deep understanding of the packaging industry, is one of the influential entrepreneurs in the industry. In November 2015, Mr. ZHOU Yunjie led in the strategic investment in the HK-listed CPMC Holdings by ORG, pioneering in the participation in the mixed ownership reform of the packaging industry by the private companies.

ZHOU Yuan

Vice President

        Mr. ZHOU Yuan, Chinese national, 29 years old, has Australian permanent residency, and graduated from Monash University with a bachelor’s degree. Mr. ZHOU Yuan has been holding the post of Vice President in ShanghaiSL and ORG since November 2010.

WEI Qiong

General Manager

        Ms. WEI Qiong, Chinese national, 44 years old, has no permanent residency outside China, and graduated from Tsinghua University with an EMBA. Ms. WEI once worked in Sichuan Chuanxi Phosphorus Chemical Group Company and Hainan Provincial Chemical Import & Export Company. She joined ORG in 1999, and has served as Director in ORG since 2008. She served as the Deputy General Manager in ORG from 2006 to 2010, and General Manager in ORG from 2010 to 2014. Ms. Wei has been deeply engaged in the packaging industry for 17 years, and overseen the listed company for many years, witnessing ORG growing into a leading player in the metal packaging industry and the largest A-share listed metal packaging company.

        Ms. WEI has deep understanding of the packaging industry, possesses rich management experience and a wide range of industrial resources, and has extensive experience and resources in the capital market. In 2014, Ms. WEI led in the acquisition of the metal packaging business units of Ardagh in Australia and New Zealand by ShanghaiSL, and the acquisition was the first of its kind for a Chinese company to fully acquire a foreign packaging counterpart.
